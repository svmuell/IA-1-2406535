AI Businesses 2025 — Idea Review

AI-powered content creation uses machine learning (large language models, diffusion models, etc.) to generate text, images, audio, video and design assets quickly and at scale. For a university student studying computer science, these tools can speed writing and prototyping, help with idea generation and documentation, assist learning (code explanations, summarisation), and raise the quality of deliverables — if used responsibly. However, the technology raises ethical risks (misinformation/hallucinations, plagiarism and academic dishonesty, bias, privacy, and deepfakes). A practical solution combines policy, technical controls, and education: require attribution and human review, use detection and watermarking where possible, adopt clear academic rules, and teach students how to evaluate and correct AI outputs. An example of AI-powered content creation app is Jasper. Jasper is an AI platform built for marketing and content generation.
